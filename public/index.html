          Tutorial

          So what is this?

          Well it's like a regular Twitch Plays, but instead of just chat controls, you can also use a keyboard or controller as well.
          To advance through the tutorial, click anywhere on screen.
          For security reasons, home, plus, capture, as well as internet access, are disabled by default, but can be used by mods and certain groups, contact a mod on the discord server if you need help.

          Join the discord server! https://discord.gg/ARTbddH

          Keyboard Reamapper

          To remap keys, click the key you want to bind, THEN click the action you want that key to do.
